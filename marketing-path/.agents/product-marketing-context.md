# WildSpotter — Product Marketing Context

> This file is loaded by all marketing skills before they start work. Keep it up to date.

## 1. Product Overview

- **One-liner:** A radar that crosses official data, context and ai to find undiscovered wild parking spots in Spain
- **What it does:** WildSpotter processes raw geographic, topographic, legal, and satellite data to probabilistically identify flat, legal, and undiscovered overnight parking spots for vanlifers. It runs a 7-stage pipeline (Radar → Terrain → Legal → Satellite AI → Context → Landcover → Score) and outputs a 0-100 suitability score for each spot
- **Category:** Vanlife/overlanding tool — sits on the "find where to park" shelf, but positioned against user-generated directories (the incumbents)
- **Product type:** Mobile app (iOS live, Android coming soon) with freemium model
- **Business model:** Free tier with full features (map, radar, scores, legal data). Premium tier planned (offline mode, satellite view, advanced filters). No ads, no hidden costs
- **Pricing:** Free. Premium TBD

## 2. Target Audience

- **Primary:** Vanlifers, overlanders, and campervan travelers exploring Spain (furgo camper owners, couples 25-40)
- **Secondary:** Motorhome families (35-55), digital nomads, Northern European snowbirds wintering in southern Spain
- **Tertiary:** Weekend warriors with converted vans, budget mini-camper travelers
- **Geography:** Spain (full country coverage for MVP)
- **Languages:** Spanish (primary), English (secondary — for international vanlifers in Spain)
- **Platform behavior:** Instagram and TikTok primary channels. No Reddit (posts get removed)

### Jobs to Be Done

1. "Find me a spot to park tonight that's flat, legal, and away from everyone"
2. "Tell me if I'm parked somewhere that could get me fined"
3. "Show me spots that no one else has shared — I want to discover, not follow"

### Primary Pain Points We Solve

1. **Massification** — Every "secret" spot on other apps now has 15 vans. WildSpotter discovers spots from raw data, not user submissions
2. **Legal confusion** — Rules vary by municipality and zone type. WildSpotter crosses Natura 2000, National Parks, Ley de Costas, and Catastro data for every spot
3. **Daily parking anxiety** — "Where do I sleep tonight?" is daily stress. WildSpotter pre-processes thousands of spots so you arrive informed
4. **Fines** — Up to €1,500 in tourist zones, €600 for Natura 2000 violations. WildSpotter shows the legal layer before you park

## 3. Positioning

### vs. User-Generated Directories (Park4night, iOverlander)

- **They** share spots people already found → leads to massification
- **We** discover spots no one has shared → uses raw data + AI satellite analysis
- **Key differentiator:** Discovery engine vs. review site. "Un radar, no una app de reviews"
- **Never mention competitors by name** — use "otras apps" or "las apps de reviews"

### vs. Google Maps Satellite View

- Many vanlifers already zoom into satellite view to find spots manually
- WildSpotter automates this exact behavior with AI + legal data on top
- **Positioning:** "We do what you already do, but faster and with legal data you can't get easily"

### Core Positioning Statement

> WildSpotter es un radar que cruza datos oficiales para encontrar rincones salvajes en España. Sin reseñas. Sin multitudes. Sin sorpresas.

## 4. Brand Voice

- **Tone:** Adventurous, bold, tech-savvy, slightly rebellious
- **Register:** Informal tú, vanlife slang welcome (furgo, pernoctar, spot)
- **Personality:** Like a smart friend who's been on the road for years and built something useful
- **Never:** Promise spots are "legal" or "safe" — we show data, the user decides. Never attack competitors by name. Never use "postureo" aesthetics — we're authentic
