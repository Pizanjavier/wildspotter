## 6. Top 5 Quick Wins (Ranked by Expected Impact)

### 1. SHIFT CONTENT MIX: 50% Mystery/Wonder, 25% Emotion, 25% Education
**Impact: High | Effort: Low**

Stop leading with pipeline/tech content. The audience doesn't care about "6 capas" until they feel the desire for what those 6 capas unlock. Flip the ratio:
- **50% mystery/wonder** — Show WHAT the radar finds without revealing WHERE. The data is the content: "87/100. Vistas al mar. 0 vecinos a 2km. Terreno plano. Legal: todo limpio." Pair with generic stock footage of the landscape TYPE (coastal, mountain, forest) — never the exact spot. Use satellite tiles with blurred/cropped coordinates. The scarcity mechanic ("quieres verlo? descarga la app") IS the product.
- **25% emotional/lifestyle** — Stock footage of the vanlife dream: coffee at dawn, dog on the beach, couple watching sunset. These scenes don't need to be YOUR spots — they represent the feeling the app enables.
- **25% education** — Legal zones, "sabías que", pipeline explainers (these already exist, just reduce frequency).

**CRITICAL CONSTRAINT:** Never reveal exact spot locations in content. This would contradict the app's anti-massification positioning. The whole value prop is "spots no one has shared" — sharing them publicly on Instagram destroys that value. Instead, tease the data (scores, terrain data, legal status) and use the mystery as the conversion hook to download the app.

### 2. CREATE CAROUSEL CONTENT (Save Machines)
**Impact: High | Effort: Medium**

Zero carousels in the feed. Carousels are the #1 format for saves on IG, and saves are a top-3 ranking signal. Create:

- "Mapa: zonas Natura 2000 en la costa española" (reference)

## 7. TikTok Specific Recommendations

TikTok is effectively a blank slate (8 followers). Before investing heavily:

1. **Don't cross-post from IG.** TikTok penalizes content that feels "polished brand" — the Remotion videos look like ads, which TikTok actively down-ranks.
2. **Go native and raw.** TikTok rewards authentic, human-first content. Best format: talking head (you or someone) explaining a spot, showing the app on screen, reacting to a wild find.
3. **Use TikTok SEO.** Captions should include searchable phrases: "dormir en furgoneta España", "pernoctar legal", "spots vanlife España", "multa acampada libre".
4. **Start with 3x/week.** All under 30 seconds. Focus on one concept per video. Build topical authority in "vanlife spots Spain" before branching out.
5. **Pin your best 3 videos.** When someone discovers you, the pinned videos are your pitch.

## 9. Content Production Notes

### Key Constraints

1. **No real spot photos available** — You can't visit spots yet to shoot original footage. All visual content must use: stock footage (Pexels), AI-generated footage (Veo), Remotion compositions with app screenshots/data overlays, or satellite tiles from the pipeline.
2. **Never reveal exact locations** — This is not a limitation, it's the product mechanic. The mystery IS the conversion hook. Show scores, data, terrain stats, legal status — never coordinates or place names that let someone find the spot without the app. At most, name a broad region ("en la costa de Almería") never a specific beach or track.

### The "Mystery as Product" Content Formula

Every "discovery" post follows this structure:
1. **Tease the data** — Score, key stats (slope, legal status, distance to nearest town)
2. **Show the landscape TYPE** — Stock footage of coastal cliff / mountain forest / desert track (matches the spot's archetype, but isn't the actual spot)
3. **Withhold the location** — "¿Quieres ver dónde? Link en bio"
4. **Drive to app** — The only way to see the real coordinates is downloading WildSpotter

This turns every post into a conversion funnel, not just brand content.

### What to produce in Remotion (high-production Reels)
- Data tease animations (score reveal, pipeline checkmarks, blurred satellite tiles)
- Pipeline explainer carousels (export as image sequence)
- Split-screen comparisons (reviews vs. data) with stock footage
- Counter animations (83,006 spots)
- Legal zone map overlays (region-level, no specific spots)

### What to shoot natively (TikTok + IG Stories)
- Screen recordings of the live app (zoomed-out map views, scanning animation)
- Voiceover reactions to aggregated data ("hemos encontrado 393 spots con score > 70")
- Behind-the-scenes of the pipeline running (terminal, maps, data)
- Lifestyle content using stock footage + text overlays

### Visual sources (no original photography needed)
- **Stock footage:** Pexels vanlife/nature library (already downloaded in public/videos/)
- **AI footage:** Veo-generated clips for specific vanlife scenes
- **App screenshots:** Live app at localhost:8081 via Chrome automation (zoomed out, no individual spots)
- **Satellite tiles:** PNOA tiles from the pipeline (cropped/blurred to hide exact location)
- **Data overlays:** Remotion-generated score badges, pipeline animations, legal checkmarks

### Carousel templates needed

- Pipeline explainer (7 stages, 7 slides — use V4 pipeline)
- "Qué mira el radar" scoring breakdown
