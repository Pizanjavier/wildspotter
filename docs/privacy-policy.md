# WildSpotter — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** April 2, 2026

WildSpotter ("the App") is developed by Javier Pizan ("we", "us"). This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we handle it.

## 1. Data We Collect

### 1.1 Location Data
When you use the "My Location" feature, the App accesses your device's GPS coordinates to center the map on your position. Location data is processed locally on your device and is **not sent to our servers**. We do not track, store, or share your location history.

### 1.2 Spot Reports
If you submit a report about a spot (e.g., "incorrect legal data", "not accessible"), we store the report category, your optional comment, and the spot identifier. Reports are anonymous — we do not collect your name, email, or device identifier with reports.

### 1.3 Analytics
We use PostHog to collect anonymous usage events (e.g., "area scanned", "spot viewed") to understand how the App is used and improve it. These events do not contain personally identifiable information. You can opt out of analytics by disabling data sharing in your device settings.

### 1.4 Crash Reports
We use Sentry to collect crash reports when the App encounters an error. Crash reports include technical information about your device (OS version, device model, app version) and the error stack trace. They do not include personal data.

### 1.5 Feedback Emails
If you use the "Send Feedback" feature, it opens your device's email client. We only receive your email address and message content if you choose to send the email. We use this solely to respond to your feedback.

## 2. Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do not require account creation or login
- We do not collect your name, email address, or phone number (unless you voluntarily email us)
- We do not track your location history or movement patterns
- We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties for advertising purposes
- We do not display ads

## 3. Data Storage

Spot data (geographic coordinates, terrain analysis, legal status, scores) is stored on our servers in the European Union (Hetzner, Germany). This data is derived from public sources (OpenStreetMap, Spanish government databases) and does not contain personal information.

Cached data on your device (previously viewed spots and areas) is stored locally via AsyncStorage and can be cleared from the App's Config screen.

## 4. Third-Party Services

| Service | Purpose | Data Sent | Privacy Policy |
|---------|---------|-----------|----------------|
| PostHog | Anonymous analytics | Event names, timestamps, device type | [posthog.com/privacy](https://posthog.com/privacy) |

## 5. Children's Privacy

WildSpotter is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## 6. Your Rights

Since we collect minimal anonymous data, there is typically no personal data to access, modify, or delete. If you have sent us feedback via email and wish to have your message deleted, contact us and we will comply.

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under GDPR including access, rectification, erasure, and data portability. Contact us to exercise these rights.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
